🤖 AI Deep Dive: Firefly & Beyond

Adobe's Firefly is not just another generative AI tool — it's a platform strategy. Unlike competitors (Midjourney, DALL-E), Firefly is embedded directly into the creative workflows of 30M+ professionals.

Firefly Revenue Model

  • Premium Credits — Users buy AI generation packs ($4.99–$29.99/mo tiers)
  • Enterprise API — Companies integrate Firefly into custom workflows (per-call pricing)
  • Training Data Licensing — Adobe's licensed content library is a moat; they license training data to other AI companies
  • Stock Substitution — AI-generated images reducing need for traditional stock photos, but Adobe captures both sides

Competitive Moat

Adobe's biggest advantage: commercial safety. Firefly is trained exclusively on licensed, public domain, and Adobe Stock content. This means every AI-generated image is commercially safe — a massive advantage for enterprise customers who can't risk copyright lawsuits from Midjourney or DALL-E outputs.
